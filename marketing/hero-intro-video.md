## AI Video Generation Prompts

The hero player is 16:9. Export at **1920×1080, MP4 (H.264)**, save as `client/public/EaseIntoAI.mp4`, and update the poster image to a matching frame.

### Option A — Master prompt (for long-form tools: Sora storyboard, HeyGen/Synthesia + b-roll, Runway workflow)

> Create a 75-second brand introduction video for "EaseIntoAI," an AI education company that teaches women entrepreneurs and small business owners how to use AI — in plain language, with no hype.
>
> **Visual style:** Warm, editorial, real. Natural light, soft shadows, authentic small-business settings (boutique, salon, home office of a coach, food truck). Diverse women aged 30–55. NOT corporate stock footage, NOT futuristic sci-fi AI imagery — no glowing robots, no blue circuit boards, no holograms. Think "small business documentary meets clean modern brand film."
>
> **Brand palette:** Near-white backgrounds (#FCFCFC), deep indigo accent (#4A42E8), dark navy text (#12161F). On-screen text in a clean modern sans-serif (Inter), lower-third style, minimal.
>
> **Narrative arc:** (1) A business owner overwhelmed by AI noise on her phone at night → (2) montage of real women running real businesses, stretched thin → (3) introduce EaseIntoAI: live, friendly Zoom webinars → (4) transformation moments: a month of content planned in an afternoon, emails that sound like her, a booking automation running while she works → (5) honesty beat: an AI draft with a wrong price gets caught by a 4-point checklist before sending → (6) live Q&A warmth, real questions in chat → (7) credibility: clean animated graphic of a 5-pillar framework and 4 fluency skills → (8) closing: the same woman from the opening, relaxed at golden hour, closing her laptop early. End card: EaseIntoAI logo, "easeintoai.co," and "Reserve your spot for the next live session."
>
> **Pacing:** Calm and confident, 2–4 second shots, gentle cross-dissolves in the montage, hard cuts for the transformation beats. Soft optimistic acoustic score.
>
> **Aspect ratio 16:9, 1920×1080.**

### Option B — Scene-by-scene prompts (for 5–10 s clip generators: Sora, Veo, Runway Gen-4, Kling — stitch in an editor)

Use the same style block for every clip:

> *Style (append to each prompt):* Warm natural lighting, editorial documentary feel, shallow depth of field, real small-business setting, diverse women entrepreneurs aged 30–55, muted warm tones with deep indigo (#4A42E8) accents, 16:9 cinematic, no futuristic AI imagery, no robots, no glowing circuits, no text in frame.

1. **Clip 1 (7s) — The overwhelm:** A woman in her early 40s sits at a kitchen-table home office at night, lit by her phone, scrolling fast through a social feed. She exhales, puts the phone face-down, and rubs her eyes. Slow push-in.

3. **Clip 3 (7s) — The webinar:** A bright, friendly live video call grid on a laptop screen in a cozy office — a dozen engaged women, one host speaking warmly and gesturing at a simple slide. Over-the-shoulder shot of an attendee taking notes and smiling.
4. **Clip 4 (8s) — Content transformed:** Close-up of hands typing a short messy note on a laptop; the screen transitions to a clean monthly content calendar filling in, post by post. The woman leans back, pleasantly surprised.
5. **Clip 5 (7s) — Automation while she works:** A salon owner blow-drying a client's hair in the foreground; in the background her laptop screen shows a booking confirmation and a reminder message sending on its own. She never touches it.
6. **Clip 6 (8s) — The SAFE Check:** Screen-focused shot: an AI-drafted client email with a price highlighted in red; a cursor corrects it; four checkboxes tick down a small side checklist; the send button is clicked with confidence.
