About EaseintoAI

Most People Feel Behind on AI.
That's Not Your Fault.

AI is everywhere right now — in the news, at work, in conversations you didn't start. And for most people, the honest feeling isn't excitement. It's confusion. Maybe a little anxiety. A quiet sense that everyone else understands something you don't.

You don't need to be a programmer to use AI. You don't need a computer science degree or a startup idea. You just need someone to explain it in plain language, show you what it actually does, and answer your real questions without making you feel behind.

That's what EaseintoAI is for. We host free, live Zoom webinars that walk you through AI from the ground up — what it is, how it works, where it goes wrong, how to use it well, and how to think critically about what it gives you.

The AI Fluency Framework

The 4 Skills of AI Fluency

Developed by Anthropic, the AI Fluency Framework identifies the four core competencies you need to work with AI effectively. Our curriculum is organized around all four.

D
Delegation
Deciding what to hand to AI
Knowing what work AI should do, what humans should do, and how to divide tasks intelligently between them.

Description
How you communicate with AI

D
Discernment

Original Framework

The SAFE Check

Before you submit anything that AI helped create, run it through these four questions. This is the framework we teach in Session 1 — and one of the most practical things you'll take away.

AI can be confidently wrong. Your name goes on the work. The SAFE Check is how you stay in control.

Learn it in Session 1 →
S
Safe to share?
Does this output contain any confidential, personal, or sensitive information that shouldn't leave your hands?
A
Accurate?
Have you verified the key facts? AI can sound completely certain while being completely wrong.
F
Fair to submit?
Is this appropriate to submit given your employer's expectations and any disclosure requirements?
E
Edited by me?
Have you reviewed and personalized it? Your name is on it. You own the outcome.
